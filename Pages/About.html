About

A talented barber with over a decade's worth of experience.
Doing undercuts, styling, long hair or short, but specializing in fades.
Starting out in Philadelphia, he has done hair and traveled across the east coast, and finally chosen to settle down in the mid-west.

Outside of barbering he does construction, web-design, and even minor mechanics.
After 4 years of apprenticeship he's a Journeyman in a local union of St. Louis.
A short 6 month sprint with LaunchCode101 set up his knowledge-base to include JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. during which he was able to make this web-site!

In the long-term he hopes to have multiple avenues of income, from being a developer, a master barber, a JourneyMan Construction worker, dabbling in stocks and trades, and even real estate. There's nothing that's going to stop him.

But ultimately it's the look on his clients face when they're instilled with new-found confidence and joy from looking in the mirror and seeing a side of themselves that they didn't know existed, and truly falling in-love with themselves that keeps him cutting hair.
